Meeting Brief

For team reviews, alignment sessions, decision meetings, and workshops that need prep plus presentation.

1/4
Meeting flow
Purpose

Start with why the meeting exists.

This starter works before, during, and after the meeting: share it as pre-read, present it live, then keep it as the record of decisions and actions.

Goal

Agree on the first wave of hybrid starters and the naming strategy for them.

Decision needed

Approve the starter backlog, or narrow scope with explicit cuts.

Presenter note: if the decision is fuzzy, the meeting will become emotional improv theater. Do not let it.
Agenda

Show the flow and keep it honest.

  1. Context recap and why hybrid starters matter
  2. Review proposed starter set
  3. Discuss naming and categorization
  4. Record approvals, edits, and owners
Discussion

Keep the important questions visible.

Question 1

Which starters must exist in the first public set versus a later wave?

Question 2

Do we document them by use case, interaction pattern, or both?

Question 3

What is the minimum shell every starter must share?

Actions

End with decisions and owners.

Decision

Ship the hybrid starter family and update the skill catalog immediately.

Owners

Assign implementation, docs update, and validation separately so nothing falls into the void.

Parking lot

Capture off-scope ideas here so they stop hijacking the core meeting.
