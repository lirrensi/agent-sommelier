Analysis

Root cause

A deployment changed rendering assumptions without updating the recovery path.

Contributing factor

The rollback playbook did not account for queued jobs already in flight.

Follow-up

Immediate fix

Patch recovery steps and verify them during the next dry run.

Long-term fix

Add pre-deploy checks that compare rendering assumptions against rollback support.
