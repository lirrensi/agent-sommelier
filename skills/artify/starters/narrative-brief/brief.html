Chapter 2

Make the stakes obvious.

Readers stay with you when the cost of inaction is clear. Spell out what changes, what breaks, and what gets easier.

What changes

Customers move from reactive work to guided outcomes in under ten minutes.

What breaks if ignored

Teams keep shipping disconnected updates, and each audience has to mentally stitch the story together themselves.

Show supporting context
  • Leadership wants a one-page summary and a deck. This starter does both.
  • Operators need details and appendix links. Those stay hidden in summary mode.
  • Presenters need one clean view at a time. Slide mode turns each section into a stage.
